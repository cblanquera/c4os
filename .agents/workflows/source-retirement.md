# Source Retirement Workflow

Use this before declaring old planning docs, briefs, pegs, notes, or imported sources obsolete.

## Read First

- `.agents/AGENTS.md`
- `.agents/context/index.md`
- `.agents/specs/manifest.md`
- The source proposed for retirement
- Records that claim to replace or supersede the source

## Process

1. Inventory the source's unique requirements, decisions, risks, evidence, acceptance criteria, and useful context.
2. Confirm every still-useful fact is represented in `.agents/context/`, `.agents/specs/`, `wireframes/`, or another current record.
3. Mark conflicting or obsolete facts explicitly instead of silently dropping them.
4. Do not delete the source unless the user explicitly asks after the no-data-loss check.
5. Record the retirement decision, retained value, and any unresolved gaps.

## Stop

Stop when the source is classified as active, superseded, partially superseded, obsolete, or unsafe to retire, and the recommended next step is clear.
