# MVP Workflow

Use this to define, narrow, validate, or freeze the smallest customer-usable C4OS product slice.

## Read First

- `.agents/context/index.md`
- `.agents/specs/research/index.md`
- `.agents/specs/research/status.md`
- `wireframes/screens.md`, when UI behavior matters

## Process

1. Identify the target evaluator and the smallest coherent workflow.
2. Record minimum capabilities, data behavior, states, permissions, errors, empty states, and verification.
3. Keep browser, terminal, extension, provider, and runtime ambitions behind explicit MVP or post-MVP decisions.
4. Reconcile accepted review, validation, wireframe, and POC learning into records before freeze.
5. Promote final shared product understanding into `.agents/context/`.

## Stop

Stop when the research records make the MVP boundary reviewable, blockers are explicit, and the next step is review, validation, POC, freeze, or implementation planning.
